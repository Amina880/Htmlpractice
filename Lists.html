Simple Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375oF (190oC).
  2. Mix all Ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Place spoonfuls of dough on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes.

Cooking Glossary

Preheat
Heat (an oven or grill) beforehand.
Bake
Cook (food) by dry heat without exposure to a flame, typically in an oven
